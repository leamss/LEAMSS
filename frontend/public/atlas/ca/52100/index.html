Library and public archive technicians

NOC 52100 ✓ Verified

Library and public archive technicians assist users in accessing library or archive resources, assist in describing new acquisitions, participate in archive processing and storage, and conduct reference searches. They are employed by libraries and public archives.

Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Library technicians
  3. Assist library users in accessing books, films, photographs, maps, documents, electronic materials and other library materials
  4. Catalogue new library acquisitions under the direction of a librarian
  5. Perform manual and on-line reference searches and make interlibrary loans for users
  6. Assist librarians in giving tours and providing children's and other specialized library programs.
  7. Public archive technicians
  8. Assist in developing inventories, forms and finding aids
  9. Implement and update classification plans and records scheduling and disposal plans
  10. Apply standards and policies for storage of records and archival materials
  11. Codify and classify archival materials
  12. Put archival materials on microfilm or in databases
  13. Transfer materials from current to semi-current status or put into permanent storage
  14. Purge, sort, reduce and sample archives
  15. Research and retrieve archival materials
  16. Participate in instructing and assisting users
  17. Ensure that proper procedures are followed in the use of computerized document management systems.
