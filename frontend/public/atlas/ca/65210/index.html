About this occupation

Workers in support occupations in accommodation, travel and facilities set-up services escort guests to their rooms, carry travellers' luggage at airports, in railway stations and aboard ships, clean and maintain public areas and passengers' rooms aboard ships and trains, and set up rooms and associated furnishings, commercial displays, exhibits, equipment and booths in facilities and establishments. They are employed by hotels, conference centres, retail establishments, passenger transport companies and throughout the private and public sectors.

Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Guest service attendants and baggage porters
  3. Carry luggage for hotel guests, escort incoming guests to their rooms
  4. Check rooms to ensure they are in order
  5. Offer information regarding features of rooms, services of hotel and points of interest
  6. Assist guests with special needs
  7. Attend to group arrivals and departures
  8. Receive and deliver items and messages for guests
  9. Handle guest complaints and requests and maintain luggage storage areas.
  10. Convey travellers' luggage at railway stations and airports by hand or hand truck and arrange for ground transportation.
  11. Ship and train service attendants
  12. Set and clear tables, serve food and beverages, clean sleeping cars and cabins, make beds, wash dishes and maintain washroom supplies
  13. Carry luggage for passengers aboard ships.
  14. Ensure customer satisfaction and comfort.
  15. Facilities workers
  16. Set-up, install, reconfigure, relocate and dismantle temporary partitions and furnishings of rooms
  17. Assemble, rotate and disassemble window displays, showcases and signage according to floor plans or instructions
  18. Assist with installation of audiovisual equipment and help in resolving problems during use
  19. May perform minor repairs on installations and routine maintenance of audiovisual equipment.
