Hairstylists and barbers

NOC 63210 ✓ Verified

Hairstylists and barbers cut and style hair and perform related services. They are employed in hairstyling or hairdressing salons, barber shops, vocational schools, health care establishments and theatre, film and television establishments. Apprentices are also included in this unit group.

Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Hairstylists
  3. Suggest hairstyle compatible with client's physical features or determine style from client's instructions and preferences
  4. Cut, trim, taper, curl, wave, perm and style hair
  5. Apply bleach, tints, dyes or rinses to colour, frost or streak hair
  6. Analyze hair and scalp condition and provide basic treatment or advice on beauty care treatments for scalp and hair
  7. Clean and style wigs and hair pieces
  8. Apply hair extensions
  9. May shampoo and rinse hair
  10. May perform receptionist duties and order supplies
  11. May train or supervise other hairstylists, hairstylist apprentices and helpers.
  12. Barbers
  13. Cut and trim hair according to client's instructions or preferences
  14. Shave and trim beards and moustaches
  15. May shampoo hair and provide other hair treatment, such as waving, straightening and tinting and may also provide scalp conditioning massages
  16. May book appointments and order supplies
  17. May train and supervise other barbers and barber apprentices.
