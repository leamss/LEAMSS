Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Examine natural, synthetic and hybrid textile, fabric, fur and leather garments or products for conformity to quality standards
  3. Inspect and grade animal hides and pelts according to size, condition and weight
  4. Verify fit or size of finished garments and products by measuring or comparing products with samples and patterns
  5. Mark defects, repair minor imperfections, cut excess materiel using scissors, and remove lint and spots using brushes, lint removers and cleaning solutions
  6. Grade and sort finished products and tag according to size, style and quality
  7. Record information on products inspected
  8. May package products.
