About this occupation

Database analysts design, develop and administer data management solutions. Data administrators develop and implement data administration policy, standards and models. They are employed in information technology consulting firms and in information technology units throughout the public and private sectors.

Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Database analysts
  3. Collect and document user requirements
  4. Design and develop database architecture for information systems projects
  5. Design, construct, modify, integrate, implement and test data models and database management systems
  6. Conduct research and provide advice to other informatics professionals regarding the selection, application and implementation of database management tools
  7. Operate database management systems to analyze data and perform data mining analysis
  8. May lead, coordinate or supervise other workers in this group.
  9. Data administrators
  10. Develop and implement data administration policy, standards and models
  11. Research and document data requirements, data collection and administration policy, data access rules and security
  12. Develop policies and procedures for network and/or Internet database access and usage and for the backup and recovery of data
  13. Conduct research and provide advice to other information systems professionals regarding the collection, availability, security and suitability of data
  14. Write scripts related to stored procedures and triggers
  15. May lead and coordinate teams of data administrators in the development and implementation of data policies, standards and models.
