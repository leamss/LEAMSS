About this occupation

Pharmacy technical assistants perform technical tasks related to preparing medications under a pharmacist's supervision. They also assist in filling prescriptions and managing patient files. Pharmacy assistants perform clerical functions and assists pharmacists and pharmacy technicians with drug dispensing tasks. Pharmacy technical assistants and pharmacy assistants are employed in retail and hospital pharmacies, long-term care facilities and by pharmaceutical manufacturers.

Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Assess prescriptions for completeness
  3. Enter clients' information and prescription details in databases
  4. Help pharmacists and other pharmacy staff with preparing, packaging and labelling of pharmaceutical products
  5. Assist in maintaining inventories of medications and pharmaceutical products
  6. Compound oral solutions, ointments and creams
  7. May process medical billing information
  8. May price and stock items on shelves.
