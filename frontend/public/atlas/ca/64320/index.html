Overview

About this occupation

Tour and travel guides escort individuals and groups on trips, on sightseeing tours of cities and on tours of historical sites and establishments such as famous buildings, manufacturing plants, cathedrals and theme parks. They also provide descriptions and background information on interesting features. They are employed by tour operators, resorts and other establishments or may be self-employed.

Day-to-day

Typical tasks performed

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Tour guides
  3. Transport or escort individuals or groups on tours of cities, waterways and industrial and other establishments
  4. Describe points of interest, answer questions and supply information
  5. Provide historical and cultural facts related to the site
  6. May collect admission fees and sell souvenirs.
  7. Travel guides
  8. Escort individuals and groups on vacation and business trips
  9. Ensure that reservations for transportation and accommodations are confirmed and that prepared itineraries are met
  10. Visit and describe points of interest and plan and carry out recreational activities
