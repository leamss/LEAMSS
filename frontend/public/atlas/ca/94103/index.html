Concrete, clay and stone forming operators

NOC 94103 ✓ Verified

Concrete, clay and stone forming operators cast and finish concrete products, operate machines to extrude, mould, press and bake clay products, and operate machines to form, cut and finish stone products. They are employed by concrete, clay and stone products manufacturing companies.

Typical tasks

  1. This group performs some or all of the following duties:
  2. Concrete products forming and finishing machine operators and workers
  3. Set up, adjust and operate single-function machines to mix, drill, grind and cut concrete to specifications in the manufacture of concrete products
  4. Check products for quality control, repair flaws and perform ongoing machine adjustments and minor maintenance as necessary
  5. Move products using forklift, crane or hydraulic hoist
  6. Record manufacturing information such as quantity, size and type of goods produced
  7. Construct clay models and moulds by hand and with hand tools for use in casting concrete products
  8. Build and repair wooden forms used to mould concrete products
  9. Position reinforcing rods or wire mesh in moulds and fill moulds with concrete from suspended pouring buckets
  10. Start vibrating tables to settle concrete, or tamp concrete using electric vibrator
  11. Remove castings from mould with crane or forklift, and finish surfaces of castings using screed or trowel
  12. Cut cured concrete pipe sections with saw or chisel, and cement sections together to form odd shaped fittings.
  13. Clay products forming and finishing machine operators
  14. Set up and operate automatic machines that mix ingredients, extrude tempered clay mixture and cut extruded clay into sections to make products such as bricks, drain tiles and porcelain insulators
  15. Operate mechanical or hydraulic ram and hot-presses to form pottery and porcelain ware
  16. Prepare and mix slip and fill moulds with slip to form pottery and porcelain ware such as jugs, cups, sinks and toilet bowls
  17. Operate kiln to bake clay products to specified hardness.
  18. Stone forming and finishing workers
  19. Operate blade or wire saws to cut blocks of stone to specified dimensions
  20. Operate machines to grind and polish surfaces of stone blocks, slabs or other stone products to specific shape or design and to produce smooth finish
  21. Operate sandblasting equipment to cut inscriptions or decorative designs in stone monuments
  22. Finish stone products with spray paint and by mounting plaques or installing concrete bases
  23. Operate stone-dressing lathe to sharpen and dress grindstones that grind logs into pulp
  24. Operate machine to drill holes in blocks or slabs of stone according to specifications.
