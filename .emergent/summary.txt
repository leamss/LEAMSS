5.  **Admin Oversight & Reporting:**

    *   A comprehensive sales dashboard with filters and export functionality.
    *   A commission report to track payable commissions for each partner.

    *   A full-featured ticketing system for users to raise issues, supporting attachments, status tracking, user assignment, and activity history.
